Network Connection via Certutil

Last updated 3 months ago on 2026-03-24
Created 6 years ago on 2020-03-19

About

Identifies certutil.exe making a network connection. Adversaries could abuse certutil.exe to download a certificate, or malware, from a remote URL.

✄𐘗network where host.os.type == "windows" and process.name : "certutil.exe" and
  not cidrmatch(destination.ip, "10.0.0.0/8", "127.0.0.0/8", "169.254.0.0/16", "172.16.0.0/12", "192.0.0.0/24",
                                "192.0.0.0/29", "192.0.0.8/32", "192.0.0.9/32", "192.0.0.10/32", "192.0.0.170/32",
                                "192.0.0.171/32", "192.0.2.0/24", "192.31.196.0/24", "192.52.193.0/24",
                                "192.168.0.0/16", "192.88.99.0/24", "224.0.0.0/4", "100.64.0.0/10", "192.175.48.0/24",
                                "198.18.0.0/15", "198.51.100.0/24", "203.0.113.0/24", "240.0.0.0/4", "::1",
                                "FE80::/10", "FF00::/8") and
  not dns.question.name in ("localhost", "*.digicert.com", "ctldl.windowsupdate.com")
